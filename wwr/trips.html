Our Featured Rafting Trips

Beginner Rapids Adventure

This gentle yet exciting trip is perfect for families and first-timers. You’ll experience Class I and II rapids, with plenty of time to soak in the stunning river scenery. Our expert guides will ensure you have a fun and safe adventure!

Highlights:

• Class I-II rapids (suitable for all skill levels)
• 3 hours on the river
• Scenic picnic spot along the way
• Ages: 6+
• Price: $60 per person

Intermediate Rapids Challenge

For those ready to take it up a notch, our Intermediate Rapids Challenge features Class III rapids with plenty of thrills. Feel the adrenaline as you navigate more turbulent waters, perfect for adventurers looking for an unforgettable experience!

Highlights:

• Class III rapids (medium difficulty)
• 4 hours of rafting
• Professional guides with safety expertise
• Ages: 10+
• Price: $85 per person

Extreme Rapids Expedition

Ready for a true test of your rafting skills? The Extreme Rapids Expedition offers an action-packed ride through Class IV-V rapids, ideal for adrenaline junkies. This high-stakes adventure guarantees non-stop excitement!

Highlights:

• Class IV-V rapids (advanced experience recommended)
• 6 hours of intense rafting
• Multiple heart-pounding sections
• Ages: 16+
• Price: $120 per person
