Utah

Utah is a state in the western United States, known for its diverse landscapes, ranging from arid deserts to towering mountains. It is home to five national parks, including Zion and Arches, which attract outdoor enthusiasts and adventurers. The state also has a strong cultural influence from the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, headquartered in its capital, Salt Lake City.
